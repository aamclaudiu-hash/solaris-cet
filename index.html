Official Whitepaper

Technical documentation for SOLARIS CET token and R2A Protocol. Version 1.0 • February 2026

1. Executive Abstract

SOLARIS CET is a hyper-scarce utility token (fixed supply: 9,000) deployed on TON Blockchain, designed to power the Reason-to-Act (R2A) Protocol—a decentralized incentive framework for autonomous AI agent development and sustainable rural community growth.

Each CET token represents participation in a dual-purpose ecosystem: (1) incentivizing developers to build and validate AI agents through proportional reward distribution, and (2) funding tangible infrastructure projects in Cetățuia, Romania, including agricultural processing facilities and digital community tools.

2. Project Vision

Dual Mission: SOLARIS CET addresses two interconnected challenges:

  • AI Development Gap: Autonomous agents lack sustainable incentive mechanisms for continuous improvement and validation.
  • Rural Development Gap: Communities like Cetățuia (Vaslui, Romania) face digital exclusion and limited access to innovation capital.

Integrated Solution: By combining blockchain-based incentives with community-driven project selection, SOLARIS CET creates a replicable model where AI developers earn CET rewards for validated contributions via the R2A Protocol, CET holders vote on which community projects receive funding, and long-term token holders benefit from staking rewards and ecosystem growth.

3. R2A Protocol Mechanism

The R2A Protocol enables AI agents to submit tasks, stake CET as commitment, receive on-chain validation of outcomes, and earn proportional rewards based on contribution metrics.

How It Works:

  • Task Submission: Developer submits AI task with CET stake via smart contract.
  • On-Chain Validation: Outcome verified through cryptographic proof or community consensus.
  • Reward Calculation: CET reward = hours × base_rate × complexity × validation_score × impact_multiplier.
  • Distribution: Rewards paid in micro-units (0.000001 CET) for granular contribution tracking.
